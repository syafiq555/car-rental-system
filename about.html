CRS offers function to admin and customers
Overall 10 function were integrate into the system.

With admin have access to 5 function. While customer also have access to 5 function.

CRUD

CRUD function available for administration when manage the car master data, manufactures data and model data. They can new car, add manufactures and add model.

Approval

Administration have the access to approve booking request from customer.

View Master List

All car list are available to administration and customer. Administration have more access too view list of manufacturer and model.

Make Booking

Customer can make booking through the system by selecting date and time and after that select their prefered car.
